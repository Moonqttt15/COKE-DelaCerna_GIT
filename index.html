Coca-Cola Bottle Evolution

From vintage glass to today’s modern contour.

Old — Early 1900s:

Vintage Glass Bottle

Heavey glass, straight-side beginnings evolving toward the iconic shape. Embosed logos and crown caps defined the earliest Coca-Cola packaging era

Classic — Mid 1900s:

Classic Contour Glass

The Famous hourglass silhouette becomes standard. Durable glass and a tactle feel made it a worldwide icon.

Modern — Today:

Modern PET Contour

Lightweight PET keeps the iconic contour while improving portability. Updated labels and closures deliver a fresh contemporary look.
